About Us

Established as a buying office in the heart of bangladesh, We cater for over 20 multinational clothing brands

Our History

Max Style Ltd. was founded in 2008 in the heart of Mount Lavinia, Sri Lanka, as a garment buying office, initially catering to U.S. clients such as Kohl’s. Starting with a small team and a monthly production capacity of 40,000–50,000 pieces, the company rapidly expanded to become a leading player in the industry, now exceeding 1 million pieces per month. With a robust distribution network through Jo Lanka and other key apparel manufacturers, combined with stringent quality assurance by Bureau Veritas, Max Style Ltd. has built a reputation for excellence and reliability. To further strengthen its global presence, the company has expanded operations to Dhaka, Bangladesh, where a team of highly trained and experienced professionals ensures efficient operations and highly competitive pricing. At Max Style Ltd., we specialize in delivering high-quality apparel for some of the world's most renowned brands, adhering to international compliance standards. We are accredited by WRAP, Accord, Sedex, and other globally recognized certification bodies, reinforcing our commitment to ethical sourcing, sustainability, and quality assurance. With a vast supplier network and a dedicated quality control team, we expertly manage the entire production process—from raw material sourcing to final shipment—ensuring seamless execution and superior product quality.

Our principles

At Max Style Ltd., our core principles define the way we operate and drive excellence in the garment sourcing industry:

✅ Integrity & Transparency – We maintain ethical business practices, ensuring honesty and fairness in all our partnerships.

✅ Quality Excellence – We are committed to delivering superior-quality apparel, meeting and exceeding international standards.

✅ Sustainability & Responsibility – We prioritize environmentally responsible sourcing, fair labor practices, and compliance with global ethical standards.

✅ Customer-Centric Approach – We focus on building long-term relationships by offering tailored solutions, competitive pricing, and reliable deliveries.

✅ Innovation & Efficiency – We continuously improve processes, adopt the latest technologies, and embrace market trends to stay ahead.

Sustainability Plans

At Max Style Ltd., sustainability is at the core of our operations. We are committed to ethical sourcing, environmentally responsible production, and social compliance to ensure a sustainable future for the fashion industry. Our initiatives include partnering with eco-friendly suppliers, reducing waste through efficient production processes, promoting fair labor practices, and adhering to global sustainability standards. We actively support the use of organic, recycled, and sustainable fabrics, while continuously exploring innovative solutions to minimize our environmental footprint. By integrating sustainability into every aspect of our sourcing and production, we strive to deliver high-quality apparel while preserving resources for future generations.
